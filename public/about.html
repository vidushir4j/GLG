WHO WE ARE

GALACTICLEGENDS GAMES was born out of one simple belief — that fun shouldn't be hard to find. Founded in the thriving tech hub of Gurugram, India, we're a small, scrappy team with a massive, galaxy-spanning vision: to build video games that bring joy, excitement, and a little bit of beautiful chaos to players everywhere.

We don't believe in corporate templates or watered-down mechanics. We build games that we want to play — packed with vibrant art, responsive controls, satisfying soundscapes, and highly addictive loops. Our flagship game, Gushout, is a testament to this philosophy: a colorful casual shooter made specifically to punch boredom in the face.

We are committed to putting Indian indie developers on the global gaming map. With every release, we continue to push technical boundaries while keeping player enjoyment at the absolute core of our efforts.
